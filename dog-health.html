Preventive Care Checklist

  • Annual vet exam & vaccinations
  • Dental check & clean as needed
  • Parasite prevention (fleas, ticks, heartworm)
  • Regular grooming & nail trims
  • Weight monitoring every 3 months
  • Behavior & mobility assessments

Nutrition Tips

Balanced Diet

Choose AAFCO-approved food with named protein sources.

Portion Control

Measure meals & avoid free-feeding to prevent obesity.

Fresh Water

Ensure access to clean water at all times.

Warning Signs

Persistent vomiting or diarrhea can signal infections or dietary issues.

Coughing, sneezing, or difficulty breathing needs prompt vet attention.

Stiffness, limping, or reluctance to move may indicate joint issues.

Parasite & Seasonal Safety

Flea & Tick Prevention

Use vet-recommended topical or oral preventatives year-round.

Heartworm Protection

Monthly medication is essential in all regions.

Hot/Cold Weather Tips

Avoid mid-day heat; provide shade & coat protection in winter.
