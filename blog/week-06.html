Crate Calmness Games Using Puzzle Toys

Use enrichment toys in and around the crate to build positive routines and less whining. This week’s guide focuses on practical buying tips so you can choose products that match your dog’s habits and your budget.

What to look for before you buy

  • Match toy size and material to your dog’s age, mouth size, and chew intensity.
  • Prioritize easy-to-clean designs so weekly maintenance is realistic.
  • Pick two toy types for rotation to prevent boredom and overuse wear.

How to use this week’s picks

Start with supervised sessions, then rotate toys through short play blocks across the week. Replace any item showing cracks, loose seams, or fragments.
