Dog Toy FAQ for Every Pup

How do I choose the right dog toy size?

Pick a toy that your dog can carry comfortably but cannot swallow. If your pup is between sizes, choose the larger option for safer play.

What are the best dog toys for strong chewers?

Look for durable chew toys made from tougher rubber or nylon and inspect them regularly for wear.

How often should I replace dog toys?

Replace toys when they crack, tear, or lose pieces. Rotating toys weekly can also keep your pup engaged.
